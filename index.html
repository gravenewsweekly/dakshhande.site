About Me

Welcome to my official website! I am Daksh Hande, a passionate aviation professional currently working with Air India Express under GFHS at Pune International Airport (PNQ).

Born and raised in Indore, I've always been fascinated by the aviation industry. At 19, I'm balancing my professional aviation career while pursuing my Bachelor's in Business Administration from K.K. Vigyan College, Indore.

My journey in aviation began with intensive training at DFly Aviation Academy, where I graduated with honors. This training laid the foundation for my current role in ground handling services.

Aviation Career

I'm currently working as Ground Staff with Air India Express at Pune International Airport (PNQ), handling critical operations including:

  • Passenger check-in and boarding procedures
  • Baggage handling and reconciliation
  • Flight dispatch coordination
  • Customer service and special assistance

My training at DFly Aviation Academy covered all aspects of cabin crew operations, safety procedures, and aviation service standards. This comprehensive training prepared me for the dynamic aviation environment.

Looking ahead, I aim to transition into a cabin crew role and eventually progress to senior positions within airline operations management.
